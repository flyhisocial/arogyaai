Always consult a registered medical practitioner for medical decisions. In an emergency, call 108 / 102 or visit the nearest hospital immediately.
  • AI chat, voice assistant, and lab-report explanations are informational tools to help you understand health topics and prepare for doctor visits.
  • Only registered medical practitioners on the platform may diagnose, prescribe, or treat, in accordance with the Telemedicine Practice Guidelines, 2020 (Board of Governors, MCI/NMC).

2. Terms of Service

2.1 The Service

ArogyaAI is a technology platform that connects patients with independent registered medical practitioners, enables appointment booking, teleconsultation, digital prescriptions, and AI-assisted health information. ArogyaAI itself does not practice medicine.

2.2 Eligibility & Accounts

  • You must be 18+ to create an account. Minors may receive care via a parent/guardian's account using family profiles.
  • You are responsible for the accuracy of health information you provide and for safeguarding your login credentials and OTPs.

2.3 Doctors

  • Doctors on the platform are independent professionals, not employees of ArogyaAI. Verification of registration credentials is performed at onboarding; however, the doctor–patient relationship and clinical responsibility rest with the practitioner.

2.4 Acceptable Use

  • No misuse of the platform: impersonation, fraudulent bookings, abusive behaviour, attempts to bypass payment verification (including cash OTP), or scraping of data.

2.5 Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, ArogyaAI's liability is limited to the platform fees paid by you in the preceding three months. ArogyaAI is not liable for clinical outcomes of consultations.

3. Privacy Policy

We follow the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian law. Health data is sensitive personal data and is handled with heightened care.

3.1 What we collect

  • Account data: name, mobile, email, city, date of birth, gender.
  • Health data: symptoms you share, appointment history, prescriptions, lab reports you upload, family-profile details you add.
  • Technical data: device type, approximate location (if you enable GPS for doctor search), usage logs.

3.2 How we use it

  • To book appointments, deliver teleconsultations, generate prescriptions, send reminders (WhatsApp/SMS/app), and operate cash-payment OTP verification.
  • AI features process your queries to generate responses; AI conversations may be stored to maintain your chat history.
  • We do not sell your personal or health data.

3.3 Sharing

  • With the doctor you book (including family-member profiles you choose to share).
  • With service providers strictly to run the platform (cloud hosting, SMS/WhatsApp delivery, payment processing).
  • With authorities when required by law.

3.4 Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data; withdraw consent; and obtain a copy of your records. Write to the Grievance Officer below. Account deletion removes personal data except records we must retain by law (e.g., financial/transaction records, prescription records as per applicable retention norms).

3.5 Security

Data is encrypted in transit, access is role-restricted, and payment confirmation uses time-limited OTPs. No system is 100% secure; report suspected breaches to the contact below.

4. Payments, Refunds & Cancellations

  • Online payments: processed via our payment partner; platform fee deducted at checkout.
  • Cash at clinic: you pay the doctor directly; payment is confirmed via OTP. The platform invoices the doctor's commission separately — patients are never charged extra for paying cash.
  • Cancellations: free up to 2 hours before the appointment; later cancellations may incur a fee up to ₹50. Doctor no-shows are fully refunded.
  • Refunds for online payments are processed to the original method within 5–7 working days.
